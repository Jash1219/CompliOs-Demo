"Heart Healthy" claim needs disclaimer

The term "Heart Healthy" must be accompanied by the standard FSSAI disclaimer regarding fat content per Claims Regulations 2018.

Rule Reference
FSSAI Claims Reg. 2018, Cl. 8
Suggested Fix
Include required disclosure: "While many factors affect heart disease..."
Formatting
Expert Verified

Net Quantity font size below minimum

The 'Net Quantity' text (500g) is 1.8mm. Legal Metrology PC Rules require 2.4mm minimum for this package size.

Rule Reference
LM-PC Rule 6(1), Schedule II
Suggested Fix
Increase font size to at least 9pt (2.4mm) and ensure high contrast.

Missing "Best Before" month/year format

The current date format only shows the day. FSSAI Labelling Regulations require full Month and Year for long-shelf-life products.

Rule Reference
FSSAI Labelling Clause 2.2.3
Suggested Fix
Format as: "Best Before: DD/MM/YYYY" or "Best Before: MM/YYYY".
